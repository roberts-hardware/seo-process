- Daylight visible through roof boards
- Major snow accumulation (risk of collapse)

### Emergency Situations
- Fire damage to roof
- Vehicle impact
- Severe vandalism
- Any situation threatening building occupants

## Emergency Roof Repair FAQs

**What if the emergency happens in the middle of the night?**
Call (724) 494-5614 anytime. We answer our emergency line 24/7 and dispatch crews immediately, even at 2 AM. Night emergencies don't wait, and neither do we.

**How much does emergency roof repair cost?**
Emergency service rates vary based on damage extent, time of day, and materials needed. We provide estimates on-site before work begins. Most homeowner's insurance policies cover emergency roof repairs and tarping.

**Will you bill my insurance directly?**
We can work directly with your insurance company. We'll provide all documentation needed for your claim and can coordinate with adjusters. However, you're responsible for your deductible.

**Do you work in severe weather?**
Safety first—always. We won't send crews onto roofs during active lightning, high winds, or other dangerous conditions. However, we can often implement ground-level emergency measures (tarping from inside, etc.) and return for roof work when safe.

**How long will emergency tarps last?**
Professional tarping, properly installed, can last several weeks or even months if needed. However, tarps are temporary solutions. We'll schedule permanent repairs as soon as possible.

**What should I do while waiting for you to arrive?**
- Move valuables away from active leaks
- Place buckets to catch dripping water
- Don't go on the roof yourself (safety hazard)
- Take photos of damage if safe to do so
- Turn off electricity in affected areas if water is present
- Don't attempt repairs yourself—let professionals handle it
