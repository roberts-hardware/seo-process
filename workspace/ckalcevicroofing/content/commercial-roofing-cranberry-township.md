## Our Commercial Roofing Services

### Flat Roof Systems

We install and repair all major flat roofing systems for commercial properties in Cranberry Township:

**TPO Roofing** – Thermoplastic Polyolefin roofing offers excellent energy efficiency and UV resistance, making it ideal for Butler County's hot summers and cold winters. TPO's white reflective surface reduces cooling costs for your Cranberry Township business.

**EPDM Roofing** – This synthetic rubber roofing membrane provides outstanding durability and weather resistance. EPDM is cost-effective and well-suited for the freeze-thaw cycles common in Pennsylvania.

**PVC Roofing** – Premium PVC roofing systems deliver superior chemical resistance and fire protection. Perfect for restaurants, manufacturing facilities, and buildings requiring enhanced safety standards.

**Modified Bitumen** – A hybrid roofing system combining asphalt and rubber modifiers. Excellent for high-traffic roof areas and loading docks at warehouses and distribution centers.

## Why Commercial Roofing Matters in Cranberry Township

Cranberry Township experiences all four seasons with intensity. Your commercial roof faces:

- **Heavy snow loads** – Winter storms can dump 12"+ of snow, stressing roof structures
- **Ice damming** – Freeze-thaw cycles create ice dams that force water under roofing materials
- **Summer heat** – UV radiation degrades roofing materials over time
- **Severe thunderstorms** – High winds, hail, and torrential rain test roof integrity
- **Temperature swings** – 100°F+ summer highs to below-zero winter lows cause expansion/contraction

Your commercial roof isn't just protection—it's a critical business asset. A failing roof means:
- Interior water damage
- Disrupted business operations
- Lost inventory or equipment
- Unhappy tenants or customers
- Decreased property value
- Potential liability issues

Don't wait for a small problem to become a business crisis. Regular maintenance and timely repairs protect your investment and keep your Cranberry Township business running smoothly.
