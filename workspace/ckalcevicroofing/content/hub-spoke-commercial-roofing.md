## Our Commercial Roofing Services

### Flat Roofing Systems

We install, repair, and maintain all major flat roofing systems for commercial properties:

#### TPO Roofing (Thermoplastic Polyolefin)

TPO is one of the fastest-growing commercial roofing systems in North America, and for good reason. This single-ply membrane roofing offers exceptional benefits for Pennsylvania businesses:

**Energy Efficiency:** TPO's bright white surface reflects solar radiation, significantly reducing cooling costs during hot summer months. Buildings in Cranberry Township and Wexford report 20-30% reductions in air conditioning expenses after TPO installation.

**Weather Resistance:** TPO stands up to Pennsylvania's harsh weather—from heavy snow loads in winter to intense UV exposure in summer. The heat-welded seams create a watertight barrier that won't fail during severe weather events.

**Longevity:** When properly installed and maintained, TPO roofing systems last 20-30 years. Our installations include:
- High-quality TPO membrane (45-80 mil thickness)
- Proper insulation layers for energy efficiency
- Professional heat-welded seams (not glued)
- 10-year workmanship warranty
- Material warranties up to 30 years

**Best for:** Office buildings, retail centers, medical facilities, schools

#### EPDM Roofing (Rubber Roofing)

EPDM (ethylene propylene diene terpolymer) is a proven, reliable synthetic rubber roofing membrane that's been protecting commercial buildings for over 60 years.

**Proven Performance:** EPDM is one of the most durable roofing systems available. It handles temperature extremes from -40°F to 300°F without becoming brittle or degrading. This makes it ideal for Butler County's temperature swings.

**Cost-Effective:** EPDM offers excellent value with lower material costs than TPO or PVC while delivering comparable longevity. For budget-conscious businesses, EPDM provides reliable protection without breaking the bank.

**Easy Maintenance:** EPDM's dark surface hides dirt and staining better than white membranes. Repairs are straightforward—damaged sections can be patched quickly without specialized equipment.

**Installation Methods:**
- Fully adhered (best for high-wind areas)
- Mechanically attached (fastest installation)
- Ballasted (lowest cost)

**Best for:** Warehouses, distribution centers, manufacturing facilities, storage buildings

#### PVC Roofing (Polyvinyl Chloride)

PVC represents the premium tier of single-ply roofing membranes. When your business demands the highest performance, PVC delivers:

**Chemical Resistance:** PVC is the only roofing membrane that resists damage from oils, greases, and many chemicals. Essential for restaurants (grease exhaust), manufacturing facilities, and buildings with rooftop equipment that might leak fluids.

**Fire Resistance:** PVC meets Class A fire ratings without additional fire-retardant chemicals. This provides superior fire protection and can reduce insurance premiums for some businesses.

**Weld Strength:** PVC's heat-welded seams are actually stronger than the membrane itself. This creates a monolithic roof with no weak points—critical for buildings in high-wind areas.

**Longevity:** PVC roofs regularly exceed 20-25 years. The material resists shrinkage, maintains flexibility in cold weather, and rebounds from impacts better than other membranes.

**Best for:** Restaurants, medical facilities, chemical plants, high-value buildings

#### Modified Bitumen Roofing

Modified bitumen combines traditional built-up roofing with modern polymer technology. This hybrid system offers:

**Durability:** The multiple-ply system creates a thick, resilient roof that handles foot traffic, equipment loading, and physical impacts better than single-ply membranes.

**Weather Sealing:** Modified bitumen creates an exceptional weather barrier. The torch-applied or self-adhering layers form a seamless waterproof membrane.

**Proven Track Record:** Modified bitumen has protected commercial buildings for 30+ years. We know exactly how it performs in Pennsylvania weather—and it performs well.

**Best for:** Buildings with high roof traffic, equipment-heavy roofs, loading docks

### Metal Roofing for Commercial Buildings

Metal roofing is experiencing a renaissance in commercial construction, and businesses are discovering why:

#### Standing Seam Metal Roofing

**Longevity:** Standing seam metal roofs last 40-50+ years with minimal maintenance. That's 2-3 times longer than most flat roofing systems. For businesses planning long-term ownership, the lifecycle cost savings are substantial.

**Weather Performance:** Interlocking panels with concealed fasteners create a watertight, wind-resistant roof. We've installed standing seam roofs that sailed through 70+ mph winds without a single loose panel.

**Energy Efficiency:** Metal roofing with reflective coatings (cool roof technology) can reduce cooling costs by 25-40%. The air gap between panels and roof deck provides natural ventilation, further reducing heat transfer.

**Aesthetic Appeal:** Modern standing seam profiles create a clean, professional appearance. Available in dozens of colors and finishes to match your building's design.

**Applications:**
- Modern office buildings
- Retail centers
- Manufacturing facilities
- Warehouses (structural standing seam over open framing)
- Slope transitions (metal works on slopes from 1:12 to 12:12)

#### Corrugated & R-Panel Metal Roofing

**Cost-Effective:** Exposed-fastener metal panels cost 30-50% less than standing seam while still delivering excellent performance and longevity.

**Fast Installation:** Pre-fabricated panels install quickly, reducing labor costs and project timelines. We can often complete installations in half the time of built-up or membrane systems.

**Versatility:** Works on a wide range of slopes and building types. Ideal for warehouses, storage facilities, agricultural buildings, and utility structures.

#### Metal Roof Restoration & Coating

Don't replace—restore. Aging metal roofs can often be restored for 30-40% of replacement cost:

- **Rust treatment** and prevention
- **Elastomeric coatings** that seal and protect
- **Leak repair** at fasteners, seams, and penetrations
- **Panel replacement** for damaged sections
- **10-15 year extended life** from restoration

## Commercial Roofing FAQs

**How long does commercial roof replacement take?**

Timeline depends on building size, roofing system, and weather:
- Small buildings (5,000-10,000 sq ft): 3-7 days
- Medium buildings (10,000-30,000 sq ft): 1-3 weeks
- Large buildings (30,000+ sq ft): 3-6 weeks

We provide detailed timelines during estimation and maintain updated schedules throughout the project.

**Can you work around our business hours?**

Absolutely. We regularly perform after-hours installations (nights and weekends) for occupied commercial buildings. Additional fees may apply for night work, but most clients find the cost worthwhile to avoid business disruption.

**How much does commercial roofing cost?**

Commercial roofing costs vary widely based on:
- Roofing system type (EPDM: $5-8/sq ft, TPO: $6-10/sq ft, PVC: $8-12/sq ft, Metal: $10-15/sq ft)
- Building size (larger = lower per sq ft cost)
- Roof complexity (penetrations, equipment, parapets)
- Removal requirements (recover vs. tear-off)
- Insulation upgrades
- Warranty levels

We provide detailed, itemized estimates with multiple options at different price points.

**What's the best commercial roofing system?**

There's no one-size-fits-all answer. The "best" system depends on:
- **Budget:** EPDM offers best value, PVC offers best performance
- **Building use:** Restaurants need PVC, warehouses work well with EPDM
- **Longevity goals:** Metal if you want 40+ years, TPO for 20-25 years
- **Energy efficiency:** White TPO/PVC for maximum reflectivity
- **Maintenance capability:** Some systems require more maintenance than others

We'll recommend the optimal system for your specific situation during the free assessment.

**How often should commercial roofs be inspected?**

We recommend:
- **Bi-annual:** Spring and fall (before and after harsh weather)
- **Post-storm:** After major weather events (wind, hail, heavy snow)
- **Annual minimum:** At least once per year even without visible issues

Regular inspections catch small problems before they become expensive failures.

**Do you offer financing?**

Yes. We work with multiple commercial financing partners offering:
- Equipment financing (roof as capital equipment)
- Lines of credit
- Payment plans
- Energy efficiency financing (for qualifying cool roof systems)

Pre-approval available, and we can work with your existing business banker.

**Will insurance cover commercial roof repairs?**

Most commercial property insurance covers storm damage, vandalism, fire, and sudden failures. Coverage typically does NOT include:
- Normal wear and tear
- Deferred maintenance
- Gradual deterioration

We provide detailed documentation to support legitimate insurance claims and work directly with adjusters.

**What warranties do commercial roofs have?**

Two types:
1. **Material warranty:** Covers manufacturing defects (10-30 years depending on system)
2. **Workmanship warranty:** Covers installation defects (5-10 years)

We offer extended warranties on qualifying projects. Warranties require regular maintenance (which we provide through maintenance programs).

**Can you repair a roof instead of replacing it?**

Often, yes—if:
- Roof is less than 75% through its expected life
- Damage is localized (under 25% of roof area)
- Substrate/deck is in good condition
- Repair costs are under 30-40% of replacement cost

We provide honest repair vs. replace analysis. Sometimes repair is smart, sometimes it's throwing good money after bad. We'll tell you which.
